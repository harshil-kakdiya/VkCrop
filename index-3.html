About VK Crop Science

Leading Pesticide Innovation

For over a decade, VK Crop Science has been at the forefront of agricultural innovation, developing and manufacturing premium pesticides that protect crops and enhance agricultural productivity. Based in India, we serve over 10,000+ farmers across the nation and export our high-quality chemicals to multiple countries worldwide.
